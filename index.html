OUR STORY

Established in year 2000, Barista Coffee Company is the pioneer of coffee culture in India. The Barita Cafe chain of espresso bars delivers a trult Italian coffee experience in warm, friendly and relaxed environment. It provides a comfortable place for people to unwind over interesting conversations and a hot cup of coffee. The Barista Coffee Company Limited has over 300+ Barista Cafe's across India including its Franchise partners, Besides India, Barista Coffee also has cafes in locations across SriLanka.
